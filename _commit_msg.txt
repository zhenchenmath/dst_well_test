Add Agarwal equivalent-time PTA plotting

The buildup Bourdet derivative against elapsed time dt naturally decays as
tp/(tp + dt), which masks "is this still IARF or have I hit a boundary?".
Equivalent time dt_e = dt * tp / (tp + dt) linearizes superposition: the
late buildup tail gets squeezed back toward tp, and the IARF plateau
extends across most decades just like a drawdown.
